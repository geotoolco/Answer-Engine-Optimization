# AEO Platforms & Tools

Purpose-built platforms for tracking brand citations in AI-generated answers, monitoring share of voice, and optimizing for AI search engines.

---

## Top Picks

**[Profound](https://www.tryprofound.com/)**
Enterprise benchmark platform. $35M Series B (Sequoia). Tracks ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini with share of voice, sentiment analysis, and prompt-level rankings. HIPAA certified. The standard choice for large teams with serious measurement needs. Starts at $499/month.

**[Gauge](https://www.withgauge.com/)**
Monitors hundreds of customizable prompts daily, integrates with GA4 to connect AI citations to actual traffic. Includes a built-in AI analyst agent that surfaces content gaps. Good balance of depth and usability. Starts at $99/month.

**[AIclicks](https://aiclicks.io/)**
Covers ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, Copilot, and Grok. Prompt cluster mapping and citation intelligence with a built-in recommendation engine called AI Lift. Starts at $79/month.
