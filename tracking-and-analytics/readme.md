**[AI Search Traffic in GA4 — SEOClarity Guide](https://www.seoclarity.net/blog/track-ai-search-traffic)**

## Key Metrics

| Metric | What It Measures |
|---|---|
| **AI Share of Voice** | % of relevant AI answers that mention your brand vs. competitors |
| **Citation Rate** | % of AI responses that link to your domain |
| **Inclusion Rate** | How often your brand appears in high-intent queries |
| **Sentiment** | Positive, neutral, or negative tone in AI descriptions of your brand |
| **Brand Understanding** | Whether AI correctly identifies your product category and features |
| **Time-to-Citation** | Days from publishing/updating content to first AI citation |
| **AI Referral Traffic** | Sessions arriving via ChatGPT, Perplexity, Copilot (tracked in GA4) |

## Important caveat

Traditional analytics (GA4, Google Search Console) only capture post-click activity. AI-referred sessions that don't result in a click are invisible to standard dashboards. This is why a dedicated AEO platform is worth it for brands where AI citation is a meaningful channel — the measurement blind spot is real.

For budget-conscious teams: start with the GA4 custom channel group setup, add [Get Cited](https://get-cited.ai/) citation reports, then evaluate whether a full platform is warranted based on AI traffic volume.
