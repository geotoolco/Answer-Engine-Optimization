# Newsletters

Curated email reads for staying current on AEO, GEO, and AI search — without monitoring every publication manually.

---

## Top Picks

**[Growth Memo — Kevin Indig](https://www.growth-memo.com/)**
In-depth analysis of AI search citation patterns and LLM behavior. One of the most data-rich independent newsletters on the topic. Weekly, well-sourced, no padding.

**[Search Engine Land Newsletter](https://searchengineland.com/newsletter)**
Daily digest covering AI Overviews updates, LLM search changes, and AEO practitioner guides. High volume but consistently relevant if search is core to your work.

**[GEO Weekly](https://www.linkedin.com/newsletters/geo-weekly-7284936478098464769/)**
LinkedIn newsletter dedicated to GEO and AEO trends, tools, and platform updates. More focused than general SEO newsletters — good signal-to-noise ratio.
