# Blogs to Follow

The highest-signal publications for tracking AEO strategy, platform changes, and research.

---

## Top Picks

**[Search Engine Land](https://searchengineland.com/library/platforms/ai-platforms)**
The publication that breaks most major AI search news. Covers Google AI Overviews updates, LLM search changes, and AEO practitioner strategy. If something significant shifts, it usually surfaces here first.

**[Ahrefs Blog](https://ahrefs.com/blog/)**
Regular AEO research with large-scale data. Publishes the most reliable CTR and AI Overview impact studies — the December 2025 study on 300,000 keywords is the most cited evidence on how AI Overviews reduce organic clicks.

**[Growth Memo — Kevin Indig](https://www.growth-memo.com/)**
One of the best independent voices on AI search citation patterns and LLM behavior. Data-driven, well-sourced, no fluff. Particularly good on the structural changes happening in search.
